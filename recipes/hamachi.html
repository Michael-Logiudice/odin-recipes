Hamachi Carpaccio

An amazingly umami dish with a blast of unique flavor!

Hamachi Carpaccio is a Japanese sushi dish that has a simple mix of ingredients to deliver a unique and delicious flavor.


The ingredient are listed below:

  • 1 Hamachi loin
  • 1/2 Red Onion
  • 1 Spring Onion
  • 3 Tbs Ponzu Sauce

  • (Optional) Cooking torch for searing

Recipe Steps:

  1. Slice Hamachin int 1/4 in. slices.
  2. Slice red onion thinly.
  3. Slice green oinion.
  4. Plate Hamachi slices on serving dish.
  5. (Optional): Use torch to quickly sear plated Hamachi.
  6. Garnish Hamachi with green and red onion.
  7. Pour Ponzu sauce over Hamachi and Garnish and Enjoy!
