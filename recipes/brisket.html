Low and slow Smoky, Juicy and Hearty Beef!

Juicy brisket with a flavorful crusty bark

ingredients listed below:

  • Prime Brisket
  • Tbs salt
  • Tbs pepper
  • 1 Tbs mustard
  • 1 cup apple juice

Recipe listed below:

  1. Trim fat from brisket.
  2. Apply mustard as binder to brisket and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Smoke brisket 1 hour per lb spraying with apple juice every other hour.
  4. Rest brisket for 4 hours then slice and enjoy.
