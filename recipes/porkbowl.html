Pork Bowl

Home

A savory and rich dish for a cold winter day!

Fatty pork with green onion and topping over short-grain rice

Ingredient listed below:

  • Pork belly
  • Green onion
  • Short-grain rice
  • Bamboo shoots
  • Soy sauce

  • (Optional) Cooking torch
  1. Roll and tie pork belly using butchers twine.
  2. Chop green onions.
  3. Steam rice for 20 Mins.
  4. Sear pork belly and cook in soy sauce braising for 1 hour.
  5. slice pork belly in discs and use torch to sear of desired. (optional)
  6. Fluff rice and srve in bowl.
  7. Place sliced pork belly over rice and garnish with green onion and bamboo shoots.
